Terms Of Use

These terms describe the baseline rules for using a Club Portal deployment. Individual clubs can apply additional booking, membership, and conduct rules to their own operations.

Last updated: April 13, 2026 | Operator: Club Portal deployment operator

Deployment Contact

Operator: Club Portal deployment operator

Support contact: Contact the platform administrator

Accounts And Access

  • You must provide accurate registration and profile information.
  • You are responsible for activity carried out through your account and for protecting your login credentials.
  • Access to some features depends on your account role, club relationship, and the deployment configuration.

Bookings And Memberships

  • Bookings are subject to club capacity, timeslot availability, and club-specific rules.
  • Membership benefits and booking entitlements only apply after the system records the purchase or activation.
  • Clubs can enforce booking restrictions for abuse, repeated no-shows, or rule violations.

Payments

Payments can run through the deployment's configured provider, including virtual checkout flows and, where enabled, Stripe-based checkout flows. A booking or membership is only treated as paid when the portal records the relevant payment outcome.

Chat, Community, And AI Features

  • Chat and community features must be used respectfully and lawfully.
  • Some chats can begin with AI-assisted responses and later be handed to club staff.
  • Harassment, abuse, fraud, or attempts to misuse automated features can result in access restrictions or administrative action.

Operational Changes

Club Portal deployments can change features, integrations, policies, and support channels over time. The live deployment operator is responsible for publishing the current contact details, retention schedule, and any club-specific policy additions that apply to the service.
