Privacy Notice

This notice describes the typical personal data handling used by Club Portal deployments. The exact contact details, third-party services, and retention schedule can vary by deployment.

Last updated: April 13, 2026 | Controller: Club Portal deployment operator

Deployment Contact

Data controller: Club Portal deployment operator

Privacy contact: Contact the platform administrator

What Data We Collect

  • Account and profile data: name, email address, password hash, role, and optional avatar data.
  • Club and membership data: club setup details, membership plan selections, membership status, start and end dates, and remaining benefits.
  • Booking and payment data: booking records, membership-linked bookings, checkout sessions, transaction records, payment status, and related identifiers.
  • Chat and community data: chat sessions, chat messages, handoff events, community questions, and community answers.
  • Client-side session data: authentication tokens, selected club state, and stored profile information kept in browser storage where the deployment uses those features.

Why We Use It

  • To create and manage member and club accounts.
  • To run bookings, memberships, payments, and related service operations.
  • To provide support, moderation, safety review, and troubleshooting.
  • To keep an audit trail for service integrity, dispute handling, abuse prevention, and system maintenance.

Third-Party Services

Depending on configuration, Club Portal can use third-party services such as Google sign-in, Google Maps, Stripe payments, and AI-assisted chat providers. When those integrations are enabled, the relevant data needed for that feature can be shared with the provider that powers it.

Who Can Access Data

  • You: through your own account views and profile settings.
  • Club staff: where needed to run the club, including member names, email addresses, bookings, memberships, and chat threads linked to their club.
  • Platform administrators: for support, maintenance, security review, deployment operations, and lawful service governance.
  • Configured processors: only when an enabled service such as payments, maps, sign-in, or AI assistance needs that data to operate.

Storage And Retention

Data can be stored both in your browser and on the backend server used for the deployment. Some temporary records such as verification codes, password reset tokens, and expired checkout sessions are cleaned up automatically. Other records such as account, booking, membership, payment, and chat history can be retained for service operation, dispute handling, security, and audit until the deployment operator applies a formal retention schedule.

Your Choices And Requests

You can update some profile information inside your account settings. If you need inaccurate data corrected, data removed, or further details about the live deployment's retention schedule or third-party processors, contact the platform administrator through the support channel provided for that deployment.
